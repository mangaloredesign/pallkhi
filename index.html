The Pallkhi, an intrecately carved and exquisitly upholstered pallnquin, was the traditional means of transport in ancient India and the previlige of the royal, the aristocrats and the landed gentry. This vehicle is carried on the powerful shoulders of the most trusted members from among the passenger's personal staff. The Pallkhi spells the ultimate in luxury, comfort and trusted service.

Pallkhi Exclusive Family Restuarant draws its inspiration from this symbol of traditioinal Indian hospitality. Pallkhi's elegant interiors, gives a feeling of being within the cosy inside of a royal pallanquin.
